  1. The information we receive will be directly run into our payment system, and thus, we’ll relieve our responsibility for examining individuals’ erroneous travel reimbursement filings.

  5. The money will be transferred as a wire transfer (from MasseyHacks Oy)
  6. If you are in doubt, turn to your bank and if they do not know, then turn to us. In this case, type in the "Additional Information" field.
  7. The recipient's name, the amount paid and the item (order confirmation and ticket, in some cases the same) should be found in the single PDF file. A picture of a ticket or other ticket showing the travel dates, the price of the ticket that you paid and the name. The amount due for the travel allowance is cannot be higher than the ticket price.

  10. The address/country you type here should be the address where you’re living and would be paying taxes.

Other recipients will be obligated to pay travel reimbursements as “tax at the source” which is flat 35 % for everyone outside of Finnish tax jurisdiction. However, MasseyHacks will be responsible for this tax obligation. Hence, other recipients will receive their travel reimbursements in full.
